About US!

Anabelle Gonzalez

Anabelle was a co-coder and researcher on this project. She is a hardworking student and is determined to go far in life. Although she doesn't know what career she would like to pursue, she will for sure be helping others one way or another. Precious Minds was dear to Anabelle's heart because she has close friends who have/are dealing with mental health problems and/or have/attempted to commit suicide. Anabelle aspires to help those who need it even if they don't know they need help yet.
