Release Journal

Purpose:

This journal is for you to let out all the things that are bothering you today whether that may be all the work you still have to do, something you're nervous about, a story you'd like to share, a memory that you love or one that you hate, or any other thing that you may need to get off your chest.

If you're worried that anyone will read this journal, don't worry no more! None of the Entries are saved to any database, so we can't see it and no one else can either. We want you to release not store.

Under the writing space a button is provided for you to release what you've just written.

Press it when you're done writing.
