Based on your answers you got... MEDITATIVE EXERCISES!

The purpose of these exercises are to help with your breathing and relaxation.

Steps:

1. Lay on your back while breathing in and out at a medium pace

2. Mentally scan your body from head to toe

3. As you work your way down your body, imagine you are releasing the stress and pressure from each area of your body
